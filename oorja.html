OORJA - The Cultural Committee
Oorja, the Cultural Committee of College of Engineering Roorkee organises various cultural events during the academic year, maintaining the norms of Indian tradition and culture as well touching global diversities of culture too. These events are organized keeping in view the students, faculty and management's collective interest of talent . OORJA proves its metal by scintillating the COER aura with the wonderful events and thrilling performances and we hope to continue this legacy of entertainment in the upcoming session too. During the organization of events, the members of committee avail opportunities of displaying resource management and corporate management skills.The Committee is run under the immense guidance of Faculty Convenors Mr. Akhil Dangwal
Event Details
ZION: is the annual Cultural Fest of College Of Engineering Roorkee (COER).It is a 3-5 day fest, and is the biggest intercollege fest organized in the college. Organized tentatively in the first week of April. Many Informal and Formal events are organized in the college during these 5 days, to extravagate the talent among the students. Zion 2015, Fuel Your Imagination was organized from 8 to 11 April 2015. The key highlights of the fest were the DJ night where Progressive Brothers, a DJ of the Sunburn performed in COER and the performance of the MTV Jugni band.
DEEPSANDHYA: Each festival is celebrated with full enthusiasm at COER, and so is the festival of lights. DEEPSANDHYA witnesses a bunch of astonishing performances, followed by fire-cracker show for the COER students.
FOUNDATION DAY CELEBRATION: Each year 3rd of December marks the foundation of COER, which is celebrated with full zest in the college,Oorja organizes the event each year.
